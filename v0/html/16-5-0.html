16.5.0 {{Staff}} module - admin settings

You can make alterations to the content of the dropdown lists within the {{staff}} management module, go to admin->admin menu and click on any of the sub-headings under the {{Staff}} Module heading.

You can make alterations to the content of the dropdown lists within the {{staff}} management module, go to admin->system administration and click on any of the sub-headings under the {{Staff}} module heading. All content is added in a list format, similar to those described in section 18.2.0 System Administration - Lists
