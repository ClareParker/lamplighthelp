8.13.0 Deleted profiles

Deleted profiles are archived, and can be restored if needed.

Once you have deleted a profile, they are archived in the system. This means that they and the {{work}} you have done with them will be included in {{report}}s. These profiles can be re-instated from the archive. To do this you will need to add them as a new user, (see section 8.4.0 Adding a profile) and click on their name as it appears in the duplicate box. When their profile appears, click on the undelete button on their profile
