19.13.0 {{Eval}}s

{{Eval}}s are questionnaires you set up in {{Lamplight}} and use to capture and analyse survey-style data.

{{Eval}}s are a major step forward for the new version of {{Lamplight}}. We'd worked closely with one of our customers to develop the initial ideas in the older version of {{Lamplight}}. {{Eval}}s, letting you capture and analyse survey-type data, are still a separate module, but is fully integrated into the core system. See section 15.0.0 {{Eval}}s module overview for more details.
