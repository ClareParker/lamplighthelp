17.6.2 Logged {{comm}}s {{report}}

From the main menu select {{report}}s -> {{comm}}s {{report}}.

A similar process to create a logged {{comm}} {{report}} is taken to that shown in section 17.6.1 Sent {{comm}}s {{report}} However you do have the ability to filter the {{report}} by {{workarea}}. Click on the downwards arrow to open up your selection of {{workarea}}s and choose the appropriate area.
