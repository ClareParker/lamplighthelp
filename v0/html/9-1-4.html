9.1.4 Completing the 'details' tab in a {{work}} record

Enter details of the piece of work.

The details tab allows you to add text information about the piece of work. The Summary field will be used on the diary and other summary listings of work. See section 4.4.0 Rich text editor for more information about using the rich text editor.

When you have added all information, click the 'save' button in the bottom right.
