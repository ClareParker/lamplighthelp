10.3.0 Viewing {{referral}} records for a particular {{person}}

Open their profile (see section 8.1.0 Finding {{people}}) and click the '{{referral}}' tab.

To view all {{referral}} records for a particular {{person}}, open their profile and view the '{{referral}}' tab (see section 8.12.0 {{Referral}}s tab).
