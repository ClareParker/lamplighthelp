23.5.0 Viewing campaign responses

View aggregated responses via the DataDirect -> {{eval}} campaigns -> view.

To view a {{report}} of your campaign responses, view all campaigns (see section 23.3.0 Viewing, edit and delete details of a particular {{Eval}}s Direct campaign), right-click (or use the menu button) on the campaign you wish to view, and select 'view {{report}} of responses'

This will take you directly to the {{Eval}}s {{report}}, set the dates and other details from your campaign dates, and show you the results.

From there you can carry out further analysis (see section 17.5.0 {{Eval}} {{report}} for more help on {{eval}} {{report}}s) and download the results (see section 17.1.4 Exporting {{report}}s).
